Education and Background

In 2021, I returned to the Borough of Manhattan Community College to complete my associate degree in health information technology. As I learned the inner workings of our healthcare system and handled patient records myself, I felt a strong sense of urgency. I began to focus less on how my education was a means to an end, and more on how it could enable me to engage with health equity on a deeper level. This desire led me to the New York City College of Technology, where I currently study professional and technical writing.
