Education and Background

My journey began at the Borough of Manhattan Community College, where I studied health information technology. As I learned the inner workings of our healthcare system and handled patient records myself, I felt a strong sense of urgency. My education was no longer a means to an end, but a foundation to engage with health equity on a deeper level. This desire to drive positive change led me to the New York City College of Technology, where I currently study professional and technical writing.
