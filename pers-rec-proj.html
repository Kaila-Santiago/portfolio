Project Overview

Skills Exercised: Copywriting, Branding, Graphic Design, Health Literacy, Research

Goal: To communicate the benefits of maintaining personal health records to patients and introduce them to MyChart, an online patient portal.

Background: I first completed this project back in Spring 2023, during my final semester at the Borough of Manhattan Community College. Since then, I have learned a lot about style guides and communication across different visual mediums, so I decided to revisit this project over a year later.

Completed Project

Brochure

To create an effective brochure, I reserached other NYC Health + Hospitals brochures such as this one from Gotham Health. Not only did this brochure help me adapt branding consistent with NYC Health + Hospitals promotional materials, but it informed the structure and content I chose to include. Firstly, the front cover communicated the most important information at a glance: the name of the program, a short description of it, and the organization.
